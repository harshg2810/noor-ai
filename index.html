Sample calls

How Noor actually sounds on a call.

Patient: I think I chipped a molar last night. Can I come in today?
Noor: Dr. Fares has an opening at 3:40 PM at the Jumeirah branch. Shall I book that for you?
Patient: Do you do Invisalign, and how much is the first consultation?
Noor: Yes. The first consultation is AED 300 and takes about thirty minutes. Would you like the next slot this week?
Patient: I want to ask about rhinoplasty pricing but I'd rather not leave my number.
Noor: Understood. Consultations start at AED 500 and the surgeon will quote during that visit. I can book you under a first name only.
Patient: My filler appointment is tomorrow. Can I push it to next week?
Noor: I see your booking with Dr. Sara at 11 AM. The next available slot is Tuesday at 2 PM. Shall I move it?
Patient: My son has a fever of 39. Which doctor should I see?
Noor: That should be paediatrics. Dr. Amani has a slot in forty minutes at the Al Barsha clinic. I'm marking this as urgent for the front desk.
Patient: Do you accept Daman insurance for a dermatology visit?
Noor: Yes, Daman is accepted for dermatology. Please bring your card and Emirates ID. Shall I book you in?
